The Mission

To develop a community at Clemson University for an understanding of Artificial Intelligence (AI) with an emphasis on Learning Opportunities, Professional Development, Ethics and Societal Implications, and Deep Learning.
-Our Constitution

The Reality

It can be hard to learn about AI. So we focus on projects and building a community.
We are working with AIRISE, NCState AI Club, and local student organisations to provide opportunities.
